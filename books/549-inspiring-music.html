Inspiring Music

I find certain music has a sense of the spiritual -- of good against evil, of God's protection, etc. It is subtle, deep, and moving. It does not have words that talk of God necessarily, but images of Christianity are typical. I find these songs or videos moving and uplifting -- better than comedy TV, let's say.

So if you enjoy this kind of genre, then try:

 

Fine Frenzy -- Stood Up

You Tube that plays song.  Second You Tube with Lyrics.

This song fires me up every day -- even though we are just 2, by standing up with grace and love, overcoming every ship they send against us, the battle will be won in the seismic shift that is in process. (God be the victor!) Just love this song!
