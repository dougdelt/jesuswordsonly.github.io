Papaya Planter - Online Discussion August 16 2010

Let's listen in to this blogger's discussion with someone defending Paul. In response to the post below, others started asking Papaya Planter whether he/she were Christian at all, and whether he/she accepted Jesus as Savior to which Papaya replied he/she had. Rather than the pro-Paul participant answer the questions, they accused Papaya of being Paul's "judge and jury." They accused Papaya of implying that God tricked us by showing the apostles approved of Paul at one point (but Revelation which Papaya quoted was subsequent). Then they accused Papaya of claiming God's word is "not inerrant" and that Papaya is trying to throw out things that "don't agree with your doctrine." Papaya supposedly has "manufactured and trumped up charges." In all of this, no response dealt properly with the arguments raised by Papaya Planter.
